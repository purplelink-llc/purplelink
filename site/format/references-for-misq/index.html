References for MISQ

MIS Quarterly accepts Word manuscripts. Paste your references below and convert them to EndNote — everything runs in your browser.

Converted output

Your references never leave your browser — there's no upload and nothing is stored.

What MISQ expects

  • Manuscript format: Word
  • Template: No LaTeX template — Microsoft Word manuscript
  • Citation style: Author-year, APA-derived with MISQ-specific conventions (e.g. “(Angelou 1969)” — no comma before the year; volume:issue written as “(23:3)”).

MISQ has an official EndNote output style, which is the practical route for Word-based authors who manage references in EndNote, Zotero, or Mendeley.

Where this comes from

Sourced from https://endnote.com/downloads/styles/mis-quarterly/. EndNote style and author-year format are corroborated via EndNote's own published style listing; MISQ's own submission-guidelines page returned an access error when this page was generated, so this is not a direct confirmation from the journal's own text.
