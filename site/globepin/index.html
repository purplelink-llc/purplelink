GlobePin

Pin the places you've been. Track the flights you've taken. Set goals for where you're going next. GlobePin puts your entire travel history on a beautiful, interactive map.

Features

Your whole travel history in one place

📍

Place tracking

Log every city, landmark, and destination you've visited. Pin them to a map and build a visual record of everywhere you've been.

✈️

Flight tracking

Scan your boarding pass to log a flight in seconds. GlobePin builds a route map of every flight you've ever taken, with stats on total distance and airports visited.

🌍

Globe visualization

See your travel history rendered on a 3D interactive globe. Spin it, zoom in, and see your pins as a spatial record of your life's movement.

📊

Stats dashboard

Countries visited, cities logged, total distance traveled, continents covered. The numbers that tell the story of where you've been.

🎯

Travel goals

Set goals for destinations you want to reach — countries, continents, bucket-list places. Track progress and watch the gap close.

🖼️

Shareable postcards

Generate beautiful travel postcards from your visit history. Share them on Instagram or keep them as personal mementos.

☁️

iCloud sync

Your travel data stays in sync across all your devices via CloudKit. Seamless, automatic, and private.

🗓️

Anniversary reminders

Get notified when it's been a year since you visited a place. A quiet nudge to return — or a reminder of how far you've traveled.
