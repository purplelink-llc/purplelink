Frequently asked questions

When does Haea ship?
Haea is expected to launch in 2026. Join the waitlist above to be notified the moment it's available.
Is there a free tier?
Yes. Haea has a free tier for core health logging and charts. Premium features ($1.99/month or $14.99/year) unlock advanced analytics including Kalman-filtered recovery state, Granger causality analysis between health variables, and biological age estimation.
Does Haea sync to the cloud?
No. All your health data stays on your device. Haea uses no cloud sync, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no advertising frameworks. Your data never leaves your iPhone.
What health data sources does it support?
Haea reads from Apple Health and integrates sleep, nutrition, weight, exercise, and biometric data including heart rate, HRV, and blood oxygen.
Is Haea HIPAA-compliant?
Haea is designed with privacy-first architecture: all data is stored on-device with no cloud transmission or third-party access. While Haea is a consumer app and not a covered entity under HIPAA, its design exceeds typical health app privacy standards — there is simply no data to breach on our end.
