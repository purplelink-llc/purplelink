Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026 · ben@purplelink.llc

What we collect

When you join a waitlist on this site, we collect your email address and nothing else. We do not collect your name, location, device information, or any other personal data.

How we use it

Your email is used for one purpose: to notify you when the app you signed up for launches. You will receive at most one email per app - the launch announcement. We do not send newsletters, marketing emails, or third-party promotions.

Who we share it with

Nobody. Your email address is stored by Netlify (our hosting provider) via Netlify Forms and is not shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party for any purpose.

How long we keep it

Your email is retained until the relevant app launches and the notification is sent, at which point it is deleted. You may request deletion at any time by emailing ben@purplelink.llc with the subject line "Delete my data."

Your rights

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email ben@purplelink.llc.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA. We do not sell personal information.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or any tracking technology. No data is collected about how you browse or interact with the site.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email ben@purplelink.llc.
