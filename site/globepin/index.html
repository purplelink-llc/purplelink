Features

Your whole travel history in one place

Place tracking

Log every city, landmark, and destination you've visited. Pin them to a map and build a visual record of everywhere you've been.

Flight tracking

Scan your boarding pass to log a flight in seconds. GlobePin builds a route map of every flight you've ever taken, with stats on total distance and airports visited.

Globe visualization

See your travel history rendered on a 3D interactive globe. Spin it, zoom in, and see your pins as a spatial record of your life's movement.

Stats dashboard

Countries visited, cities logged, total distance traveled, continents covered. The numbers that tell the story of where you've been.

Travel goals

Set goals for destinations you want to reach - countries, continents, bucket-list places. Track progress and watch the gap close.

Shareable postcards

Generate shareable travel postcards from your visit history. Share them on Instagram or keep them as personal mementos.

iCloud sync

Your travel data stays in sync across all your devices via CloudKit. Syncs automatically. Stays private.

Anniversary reminders

Get notified when it's been a year since you visited a place. A quiet nudge to return - or a reminder of how far you've traveled.

Frequently asked questions

When does GlobePin ship?
GlobePin is the closest to launch of Purplelink's three apps, currently in active testing at build 79+. Expected App Store release in 2026 - join the waitlist to be notified.
How do I log a flight?
Tap the + button and enter your departure and arrival airports. GlobePin calculates the route, distance, and duration, then adds the flight to your map, stats, and timeline automatically.
Does GlobePin sync across devices?
Yes. GlobePin uses iCloud sync via CloudKit - Apple's own infrastructure - so your travel data is available across all your iOS devices without a separate account or subscription.
Is there a free tier?
Pricing details will be announced at launch. Join the waitlist to be notified.
What's the difference between GlobePin and other travel trackers?
GlobePin tracks the complete travel record: every place visited, every flight taken, and every destination on the list. It combines a 3D globe view, detailed flight statistics, travel goals, anniversary reminders, and exportable travel postcards - all synced via iCloud, no social network required.
