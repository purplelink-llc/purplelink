How packs work

  1. Pay once at checkout. Stripe captures your email.
  2. Our backend mints 5 (or 20) review tokens and emails them all to you immediately.
  3. Hand tokens out to students, co-authors, or use them yourself. Each token is good for one Standard Paper Review.
  4. To use a token: open the token-use page or paste it directly into the upload form.

FAQ

Do tokens expire?
No. Pack tokens have no expiry date.
Can I use pack tokens for Deep / Anonymity / Journal Pack tiers?
Pack tokens are for the Standard tier (which already includes Anonymity Check free). To get the Journal Pack or Deep two-pass tier, buy those directly at $11 or $15.
What if I lose the email with my tokens?
Email ben@purplelink.llc with your Stripe receipt and we'll resend the token list.
Can I get an invoice for institutional reimbursement?
Yes — request an invoice when you complete the purchase, or email ben@purplelink.llc with your Stripe session ID afterward.
