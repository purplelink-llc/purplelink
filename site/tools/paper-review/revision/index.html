Revision Review

Did your revisions actually address the original Paper Review's findings? Upload the revised manuscript + the Markdown report you downloaded last time. We'll tell you which checklist items you fixed, which you missed, and whether any new problems were introduced.

Requires the Markdown report from your previous review. Save it before you close the page next time.

What the report includes

  1. Address Tracker. Every item from the original Rectification Checklist is marked: addressed / partially / not addressed / not evaluable, with a quote from your revision showing the fix where applicable.
  2. New Issues. Sometimes fixing one thing breaks another (a new experiment whose stats weren't run; a trimmed paragraph that removed a key caveat). The Revision Review flags up to five new issues, with severity.
  3. Action Checklist. What still needs to change before resubmission.

FAQ

What if I lost the original Markdown report?
Run a fresh Paper Review on the revised manuscript (from $9) instead. We can't recover prior reviews — they're deleted from our servers on retrieval.
Why is this cheaper than a full review?
A revision review reuses most of the analysis you already paid for, so the COGS is lower. The output is also tighter — it's an evaluation against the original review, not a fresh red team.
